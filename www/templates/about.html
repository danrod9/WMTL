About the Tech Live event

Walmart Tech Live: Imagine the Future, is a half day morning event focus in technology. Since Walmart has relied on disruptive tech to distance us from our competitors, we have selected a few expert presenters of key future strategic technical focus areas (external and internal) to ignite the learners in our organization at all levels and inspire a new generation of innovators.

Walmart Tech Live will have breakout sessions that will be focused on those associates with specific needs or desires to learn more in that specific area.

About the Tech Live app

Tech Live Mobile App is the official event app for Walmart, it gives you access to the all-day Walmart Tech Live agenda; it provides detailed information about the content and the speakers, and allows you to save your favorite talks to your calendar.



For more information, questions and concerns email: techlive@walmart.com
